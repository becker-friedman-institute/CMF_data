Census of Manufactures Data

This project, led by scholars Anders Humlum, Rick Hornbeck, and Martin Rotemberg (NYU), involves digitizing the U.S. Census of Manufactures (CMF) from 1850, 1860, 1870, and 1880, capturing all existing establishment-level records.
